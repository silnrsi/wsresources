The Fixedsys Excelsior font includes the Gothic characters that are allocated to
codepoints in the Private Use Area by the author.

The original Fixedsys Excelsior author said in the scripts page and image:
Gothic is included in Unicode but in the SMP, which means all kinds of problems.  So I
placed it in the private use range, where people could actually get to it.
